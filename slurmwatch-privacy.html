Privacy Policy for SlurmWatch

Last updated: January 10, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how SlurmWatch ("we", "us", or "our") handles your information when you use our mobile and desktop application.

1. Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or share any personal information.

SlurmWatch is designed as a client-side tool to interface with your own servers. We have no access to your data.

2. Server Credentials

Your server credentials (hostnames, usernames, passwords, and private keys) are:

  • Stored securely on your device using the iOS/macOS Keychain.
  • Used solely to establish a Secure Shell (SSH) connection directly between your device and your specified server.
  • Never transmitted to us or any third-party servers.

3. Usage Data

We do not use any third-party analytics services (e.g., Google Analytics, Firebase) to track your usage of the app.

4. Network Communication

The app communicates only with:

  • The servers you explicitly configure via SSH.
  • Apple's App Store services (solely for validating in-app purchase receipts, handled by iOS/macOS).

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via our GitHub repository: https://github.com/syseitz/SlurmWatch

SlurmWatch is an open-source project.
